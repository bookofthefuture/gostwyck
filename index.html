Our Heritage

Founded in the swinging sixties, Gostwyck Motors has been a symbol of British automotive excellence for over 60 years. From our original petrol-powered sports cars that captured the spirit of an era, to our modern electric interpretations, we have always stayed true to our founding principles.

Today, we combine our rich heritage with sustainable innovation, creating electric vehicles that honor the past while embracing the future. Each Gostwyck car is still hand-finished in our original factory in the North of England.
